## 🎬 Why Use This Tool?
Social media platforms like **Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts** heavily compress your videos. If you upload in 1080p, they make it look blurry. 

**The Secret:** If you upscale your video to **4K** before uploading, the algorithms (like Instagram's) treat it as high-quality content and give it a "Quality Boost," helping your videos go viral on the Explore/Shorts feed.

## ✨ Key Features
* **4K Video Upscaling:** Inject pixels into your 1080p footage using advanced Lanczos filtering.
* **Smart Sharpening:** Fix out-of-focus or blurry videos for a crisp, professional look.
* **MP3 Audio Extraction:** Strip 320kbps high-fidelity audio from any video file instantly.
* **100% Offline Privacy:** Your videos never leave your device. All processing happens in your browser.
* **No File Size Limit:** Since there is no upload, you can process large video files easily.

## 🛠️ How it Works
This tool uses **WebAssembly (WASM)** to run the world-class **FFmpeg** engine directly inside your web browser. This allows professional-grade video editing without needing to install heavy software like Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve.

### Steps to Enhance:
1. Drag and drop your video file.
2. Select **Enhance Video to 4K**.
3. Choose your **Enhancement Level** (Smart or Aggressive).
4. Click **Start Processing**.
5. Once finished, your 4K video will download automatically.
